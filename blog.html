Essential Trading Tips

Risk Management

Never risk more than 2% of your capital on a single trade. Proper risk management is the key to long-term success.

Follow the Trend

"The trend is your friend" - Always trade in the direction of the market trend, not against it.

Emotion Control

Keep emotions out of trading. Stick to your strategy and don't let fear or greed drive your decisions.

Position Sizing

Size your positions appropriately for your account size. Avoid over-leveraging.

Keep a Journal

Document every trade - entry, exit, reasoning. This helps identify strengths and weaknesses.

Watch the News

Stay updated with economic calendars. Market movements often follow news events.
